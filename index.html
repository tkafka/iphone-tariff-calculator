Calculation Method:
Final Price = (Base Price × BOM %) + (Base Price × Other Costs %) + (Base Price × Margin %) + (Tariff on BOM)
Where Tariff on BOM = (Base Price × BOM %) × Tariff Rate

Where:
  • BOM %: Cost of components as % of Base Price (Default: ~51% iPhone, ~46% Watch, ~56% MacBook, ~57% Mac)
  • Other Costs %: Other costs as % of Base Price (Default: ~12% iPhone, ~13% Watch, ~11% MacBook, ~11% Mac)
  • Margin %: Profit margin as % of Base Price (Default: ~37% iPhone, ~41% Watch, ~33% MacBook, ~32% Mac)
  • Tariff Rate: U.S. import tax applied *only* to the BOM Cost portion.

Example (iPhone 16 Pro - Base $999, Tariff 25%):
Defaults: BOM 51%, Other 12%, Margin 37%
BOM Cost = $999 × 0.51 = $509.49
Other Costs = $999 × 0.12 = $119.88
Margin = $999 × 0.37 = $369.63
Tariff Cost = $509.49 × 0.25 = $127.37
Subtotal (Defaults) = $509.49 + $119.88 + $369.63 = $999
Retail = Subtotal + Tariff Cost = $999 + $127.37 = $1126.37
Final Rounded Price ≈ $1199
